Zombie vs HeMan game

Requirements

1. System will start with a he-man and three zombies.
2. Zombies will be present at random locations.
3. Food items will be added to screen at random places time to time.
4. Sword and its handle will be placed at different places.
5. When HeMan collects sword and handle, he can kill a zombie with that.
6. FIrst aid kit will be placed randomly and when he-man gets that, even after colliding with zombie, he will not die.
7. If zombie collides with heMan he man will die.
